Safety together, safety in numbers

AlertTown is a movement for improving quality of life through real time sharing of information amongst citizens.   

If you see a significant event, report it using AlertTown.  In return receive real time information on events around you from other users of AlertTown.   AlertTown makes its best attempt to validate information received and provides important alerts for a period of time when the risk of related events is the highest.

Together lets make our communities safer.
